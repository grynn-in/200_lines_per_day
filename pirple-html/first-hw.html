Channa Masala is my favourite dish to eat with chapati or rice

The list of ingredients to make chane masala are as follows

I will leave the making of rice / chapati to you

  1. 2 cups of chana
  2. 2 tomatoes
  3. 2 onions
  4. 2 cloves of garlic
  5. A piece of ginger
  6. channa masala poweder
  7. fourth

Here is the method

  • Soak the chickpeas (channa) overnight (roughly 8 hours) and then boil till they are soft
  • Set boiled channa aside
  • fry onion, ginger and garlic in a tbl. spoon of oil
  • Add diced tomatoes and bring it to a boil
  • Add channa masala poweder (store purchased)
  • Add boiled chickpeas to the pan
  • Boil for antoher 5-8 min till everything combines
  • Add salt to taste
